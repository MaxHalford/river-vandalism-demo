Live Wikipedia vandalism detection

River online learning vs. daily-retrained LightGBM vs. Wikimedia's Lift Wing. · source

How this works

What you're looking at

A live demo of online machine learning predicting which Wikipedia edits will be reverted. Three models race in real time on every edit that flows through Wikimedia's EventStreams:

  • River online — logistic regression with a standard scaler. Calls learn_one(x, y) on every labeled edit. No retraining, adapts to drift naturally.
  • scikit-learn batch — LightGBM retrained every … hours on the last … days of labeled data. The "classical" baseline.
  • Lift Wing — Wikimedia's own production model revertrisk-language-agnostic (XGBoost). The benchmark we're not trying to beat.

How labels work

Every edit gets all three predictions the moment it arrives. The label arrives later:

  • Positive: Wikimedia attaches the mw-reverted tag to the revision (we watch the revision-tags-change stream for this).
  • Negative: no revert tag arrives within … hours of the edit. The WMF Research convention is 48 hours.

Both timestamps are recorded — when the edit happened and when the label became knowable. That's what makes honest backtesting possible (River calls it delayed progressive validation).

The metrics chart

Rolling AUC / log-loss / Brier over the last 2,000 labeled events, recomputed every minute by the ml service.

  • ROC-AUC is undefined when only one class is present — stays blank until the first negative label, ~…h after launch.
  • log-loss and Brier are well-defined from the first labeled edit.

The live event stream

Every edit the ingest service sees, with all three model scores and the eventual label as it arrives. Updates over WebSocket; falls back to polling every 5s if WS is blocked.

Autoresearch

When enabled, once a day a gpt-5 call proposes a new River pipeline (and optionally a new featurizer). The candidate runs in an AST-sandboxed subprocess against the last 48h of recorded history. A paired bootstrap on per-prediction log-loss decides whether to hot-swap it into production: ΔAUC > 0.005 AND p < 0.05.
